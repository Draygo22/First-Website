About Us

Interesting Facts

Since 2013, Sound'O Sonic has been serving musicians, collectors and friends who want honest advice and first hand knowledge.
We are passionate about what we sell and who we sell to, whether you talk to us over the phone about a Duesenberg Starplayer
come to the store to check out a new Drum Kit, or email us about a Marshall Amp.



Our Partners

At Sound'O Sonic we have a very close relationship with every associated music industry brand.
We are the sole distributor and importer of Duesenberg, Alvarez, D'Angelico, Lakewood, Furch, Danelectro, and Zemaitis Guitars.
We also pride ourselves in being the only stockist of Magnatone Amplifiers in Wakanda.
